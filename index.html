Abuja

Abuja, located in central Nigeria, lies between the country’s northern and southern regions. It sits about 760 km from Lagos and 480 km from Kano, serving as a central hub connecting major parts of West Africa. Known for its modern design and political importance, it is one of Africa’s fastest-growing capitals.
