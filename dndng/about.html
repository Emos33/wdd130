Daily Need Distributors Ltd

History

Daily Need Distributors: Was registered a limited liability company on June 22nd 1999.
Managing Director : Abdulrahman Mohamed worked for A.B. Chami for 17yrs who were P&G distributors.
He started selling P&G Brands in 1994 in Yola as a Van Sales Rep and set up the Chami Operations in Kano and Jos. Resigned a Senior Manager 1999.
He left in 1999 to work as a strategic Sub Distributor to Nwanna & Sons a former distributor of P&G.
DND Started Business negotiations and operations with P&G on 3-May-1999 and signed distributorship agreement with P&G on 1-Mar-2002.


DND's Philosophy is to build a long term profitable business relationship with P&G and other principals.
DND is one of the fastest growing P&G distributors in West Africa. DND won 8 out of 12 location performance awards for the year 2012/2013, and currently one of the biggest P&G distributors in Nigeria with best performing Regional Distributor Award in Modern Trade in 2018 and emerged as the Regional Distributor of the year 2018/2019. Currently we have attained annual turnover of NGN10.6 billion FY ending June 2023.
DND has centralized its operation in Abuja metropolis by acquiring a central warehouse which will serve as its headquarters located at Idu Industrial Area, Abuja. Phase 1 is completed and in use as at February, 2019 while there is room for future expansion of 3,000 sqm warehousing space.
